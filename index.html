Dr. F. Story Musgrave

The man, the myth, the legend.
Dr. Story Musgrave was an astronaut-physician who rode on the spaceshuttle seven times and was the oldest astronaut in space.

    A timeline of Story Musgrave's life:

  • 1935 - Born in Boston, MA
  • 1953 - A car accident led to him dropping out of high school and enlisting in the US Marine Corps as an aircraft mechanic. He completed his GED in the USMC.
  • 1958 - He graduated from Syracuse University with a B.S. in Mathematics & Statistics.A year later he received an M.B.A. in Computer Programming at UCLA. During this time he was also employed as an Operations Analyst by the Eastman Kodak Company in Rochester, NY.
  • 1960 - Garnered an interest in the nervous system and completed a B.A. in chemistry at Marietta College and was accepted into Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons.
  • 1964 - Musgrave finished his M.D. and served as an intern in General Surgery at the University of Kentucky Medical Center. He continued there as a USAF postdoctoral fellow conducting research in aerospace medicine.
  • 1966 - Became interested in the new scientist-astronaut program rolled out by NASA, applied, and was selected in NASA Astronaut Group 6. After completing training
  • 1967-1983 - After Musgrave's selection to the 6th Astronaut Group, NASA funding was cut and his groupmates were given the opportunity to leave. Most left the group but Musgrave stayed on and waited 16 years for an assignment. Becoming the only astronuat to serve on five Space Shuttle missions and holding the record for oldest astronaut in space before John Glenn.
